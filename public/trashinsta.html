Complete Guide: How to Download Your Instagram Data

Instagram provides users with the ability to download their personal data as part of privacy regulations like GDPR. This data includes your followers, following lists, posts, messages, and more. Here's a comprehensive step-by-step guide to help you get your data safely and securely:

Step 1: Access Instagram Settings

Open Instagram on your mobile device or visit instagram.com on your computer. Navigate to your profile by tapping your profile picture in the bottom right corner. Then tap the three lines (hamburger menu) in the top right corner and select "Settings".

Step 2: Navigate to Privacy and Security

In the Settings menu, scroll down and look for "Privacy and Security" or "Account". The exact location may vary depending on your app version. This section contains all privacy-related options including data download.

Step 3: Find Download Your Information

Look for "Download Your Information" or "Data Download" option. This feature allows you to request a copy of all your Instagram data, including photos, videos, messages, and importantly, your followers and following lists.

Step 4: Select Data Types

Choose "Some of your information" rather than "All available information" to get a smaller, faster download. Specifically select "Followers and Following" as this is what our tool needs to analyze your non-followers.

Step 5: Choose Format and Request

Select HTML format (recommended) as it's easier to process than JSON. Choose your preferred date range - usually "All time" gives you the complete picture. Submit your request and wait for Instagram to prepare your data.

Step 6: Download and Upload

Instagram will email you when your data is ready (usually within 48 hours). Download the ZIP file and upload it directly to our tool for instant analysis!
